# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a respectful and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful
- Provide constructive feedback
- Respect the work of others

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Offensive language
- Spam or malicious activity

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or restrict users who violate these guidelines.
